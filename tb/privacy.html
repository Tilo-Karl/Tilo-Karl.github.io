Privacy Policy for True Blocker

Last updated:

Summary

  • No tracking, no analytics, no ads.
  • Uses Apple’s Call Directory Extension to block Swedish telemarketing range 075-557.
  • No personal data leaves your device. We don’t collect contacts, call logs, or your number.

What the app does

True Blocker installs a local block list via Apple’s Call Directory to block calls from the Swedish telemarketing range 075-557. Blocking happens on-device.

Data handling

  • On-device only.
  • No personal data collection.
  • No servers, no telemetry.

Third parties

No third-party SDKs, analytics, crash reporters, or ads.
